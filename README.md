### About

Servus, I'm Abhi.

I'm a Data & AI Engineer, but really I'm the person who asks *"why are we doing it this way?"* and then just starts doing it better. I don't like unnecessary complexity. If a pipeline needs three people to understand it, it's too complicated. My rule of thumb: **robust enough to keep running, simple enough to still make sense at 2 a.m.**

Technically I'm at home in Python, SQL, Azure, and everything real-time. Domain-wise I care most about energy and sustainability — data that actually moves something in the real world.

Off-by-one errors are funny. Not missing deadlines is funnier.
